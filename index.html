Scientific Explanation

Our product works by condensation and distillation; air conditioners absorb heat and moisture from the environment so that the air feels cool, the heat keeps the water from condensing, but once it cools off it forms water droplets

Sustainable Development Goal

Our product is directly influenced by goal number 11 which hopes to create sustainable cities and communities, this is because we hope to reduce water wastage in communities from urban, rural and anything in between

Read More

During the development of our product we investigated how required its use would be, and we realized that for each average house, 2 to 5 liters of water are thrown per hour only from the refrigeration system, now take into account the number of houses that there are in a city, and especially in a city like Hermosillo, where refrigeration is used for a large part of the year.

To use it first connect the tank to the air conditioning, then the water that condenses inside the air conditioner will pass through a tube and these will be filtered by a fabric that will prevent any residue or soil from passing. The tank has a water tap to obtain water by that method or it can also be taken out by another tube that will be directed to an irrigation system
